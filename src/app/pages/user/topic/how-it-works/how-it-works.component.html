How Topic Submissions Work

Before You Submit a Topic:

  • All topics are moderated

    We review every submission to ensure it aligns with our community guidelines.

  • Topics aren’t added automatically

    If your submission is approved, it will appear on the map after moderation.

  • We may reach out

    In some cases, we’ll contact you for additional information or clarification.

By submitting a topic, you agree to our guidelines and understand that your topic may be rejected if it doesn’t meet our criteria.
